About iCODEclub

iCODEclub is Sonrise High School's premier coding and technology club. We are a community of passionate students who explore the world of programming, web development, and technology innovation. Our mission is to empower students with technical skills and foster a culture of innovation and ethical hacking.

What We Do

Programming

Learn and master various programming languages through hands-on projects and challenges

Coding

Develop practical coding skills through real-world applications and problem-solving

Ethical Hacking

Explore cybersecurity concepts and ethical hacking techniques in a safe, educational environment

Innovation

Develop creative solutions to real-world problems
